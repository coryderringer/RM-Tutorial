Within-Subjects Quiz

Please answer if the following statements are TRUE or FALSE

You are conducting a study to test a new IQ-boosting drug. In your study, some of the participants receive the drug and some receive a placebo. This is a between-subjects design, because not everyone receives every level of the independent variable.


Correct Answer: TRUE. Between-subjects designs assign one level of the IV to each participant, whereas within-subjects designs require every participant to receive every level of the IV. In this case, because each participant only received either the drug or the placebo, it is a between-subjects design.


Within-subjects designs are true experiments, whereas all between-subjects designs are correlational.


Correct Answer: FALSE. Whether or not a design is a true experiment is related to random assignment, not whether the design is between-subjects or within-subjects. Either design can be a true experiment.


Between-subjects designs eliminate noise in the data from individual differences, increasing statistical power.


Correct Answer: FALSE. It's actually within-subjects designs that control for individual differences!
